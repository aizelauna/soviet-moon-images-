Broadway

Tall brick buildings jab up into the evening haze and disappear. Most look abandoned, snow entering broken windows, but to the east is a high-rise apartment complex that looks like it is still being attended to. A sign above the door says LABRY APARTMENTS. To the west is the tallest office building in the city, a beautiful art-deco structure of marble and stainless steel. To the northwest is Bellona Auditorium, and to the northeast stands the O.o.f Theater.

Broadway continues to the north and south.
