Broken Bottle Cafe

The bright light in the right-most corner reveals that the seemingly black walls are really dark green. Surprisingly clean brown/tan paisley cloth covers the ceiling and floor. A lone broken bottle is lying in the far left corner. Along the east wall above the door there is a sign that says EXIT.

Vampy, Patroclus, and Rebis are here.

They seem to be having a conversation.
