Broken Bottle Cafe

Rebis
Rebis is the strange conjunction in one body of three entities: a female human, a male human, and a mysterious negative energy being. A greenish glow escapes from behind tightly-wrapped bandages that swathe em from head to foot. E wears fashionable high-heeled boots, and sunglasses float a few inches before eir eyes. As you might expect, Rebis tends to be withdrawn, brooding, and self-obsessed at times, but e still likes to laugh and make atrocious puns. A dedicated postmodern, e believes that passion and irony are indissociable.
E is awake and on his feet.

The bright light in the right-most corner reveals that the seemingly black walls are really dark green. Surprisingly clean brown/tan paisley cloth covers the ceiling and floor. A lone broken bottle is lying in the far left corner. Along the east wall above the door there is a sign that says EXIT.

Vampy, Patroclus, and Rebis are here.

They seem to be having a conversation.
