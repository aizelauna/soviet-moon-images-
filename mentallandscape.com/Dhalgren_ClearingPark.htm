Clearing In The Park

You are standing in a large clearing in the park. There is a fountain here, some picnic tables, and the ground is covered with a number of bedrolls and sleeping bags. This seems to be a place where anyone is free to make their home. A cinderblock furnace has been erected in the middle of the clearing. A fire is burning in it. It melts a patch of snow around it. A gravel path extends to the east and west. To the north are some public restrooms.

The steady beat of music can be heard from the southwest, beyond a hill. To the south, through a tangle of chokecherry and barrier wire, you see an abandoned road running east to west.
