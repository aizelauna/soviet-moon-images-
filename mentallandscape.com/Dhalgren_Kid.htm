Scorpion Nest

Kid
Tall and thin, with longish black hair and strong cheekbones, and a niobium earring in his left ear. He is wearing an open leather vest, letting you see a slightly-hairy chest, and glimpse the flash of a steel ring through his right nipple. The nails on his long slender fingers are slightly bitten, and his bare foot seems a bit dirty.
He is sleeping.
Carrying:
Brass Orchid

The paint is peeling and some of the plaster has fallen, but this place seems to be in fairly good shape. There is a dense, cloying smell of unwashed people and burnt food, but it makes you feel strangely comfortable and at home. You glance at the door just in case a quick getaway necessary.
