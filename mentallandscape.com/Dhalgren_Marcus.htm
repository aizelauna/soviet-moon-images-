Marcus
Dark on the hide of a fore-limb is a marking, like two snakes coiled--an eternal braid.
Taut, Marcus glances around in the daylight.
He is shaggy, tawny, toothy, striped.
He is curious and quick to smile.
Around his neck is a necklace of knucklebones.
On a finger Marcus wears a platinum half-moon ring... Light blazes luminesce.
He is sleeping.
Carrying:
Project Proposal, Chorus Costume, leather bracelet, a torn leaf, a printed page, Fly on the Wall, Coyote costume, Vannevar Bush costume, a scrap of scripture, a scrap of poetry, thought transformation

From here you can see the doors leading out and the stage.
You see Playbill here.
Marcus (sleeping> is here.
