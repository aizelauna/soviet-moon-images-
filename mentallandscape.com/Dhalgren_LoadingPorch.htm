Loading Porch

The streetsign on the corner lamppost says "Broadway". Across the icy street to the west you see a loading porch. From their tracks under the awning, four- and six-foot butcher hooks swing gently -- though there is no wind. A fire escape, above a street lamp pulsing slow as a failing heart, looks like charred sticks, some still aglow.

The street continues to the north and to the south.
