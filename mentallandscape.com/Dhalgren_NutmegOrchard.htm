Nutmeg Orchard

A snow-covered concrete path runs east and west through a peaceful and startlingly beautiful orchard of nutmeg trees. A canopy of leaves arches over several benches of cedarwood. The trees are laden with sweet yellow nutmeg fruit, and some green monkeys play in the upper branches. Paper lanterns have been lit and hung from some of the trees.

You notice a faded small sign by the path. It reads, "Please do not feed, pet, annoy, harass or kick the animals." A narrow path leads south.
