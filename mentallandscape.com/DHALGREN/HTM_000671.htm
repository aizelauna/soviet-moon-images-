O. The Crocodile (#671)

(an instance of Generic Post-Apocalypse Room made by Calkins)

     You can see here a blind man carrying a full begger's wallet about to collide with a broken obelisk, on which a crocodile is waiting with open jaws. This blind man is the symbol of he who makes himself a slave of material things. His wallet is packed with his errors and his faults. The broken obelisk represents the ruin of his works; the crocodile is the emblem of fate and the inevitable Expiation.
