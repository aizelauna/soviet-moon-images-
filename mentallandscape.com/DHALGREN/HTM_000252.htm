The Parlour (#252)

(an instance of Generic Post-Apocalypse Room made by Pierce)

     You have entered the Parlour of the Chantry. In this room, people meet, socialize and relax. There are several couches, all of the large, victorian back, and a couple of chairs. The colours of the room are dark oak, red velvet and cream, and the carpeting is thick. In the south of the room stands a large fireplace and hearth, which always has a cheery blaze burning.
