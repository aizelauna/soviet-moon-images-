Map of Africa (#1035)

(an instance of generic thing made by Ibn_Khaldun) 

     A huge map, painstakingly charted by hand. You can dimly see that
     it is vividly colored. It is covered with handwritten notations
     and scrawled messages in Swahili, English, Xhosa, Hausa, Kikuyu,
     French, Herero, Tuareg, Twi, Zulu, Arabic, Shona, Afrikaans, and a
     host of other languages; even in the languages which you know, you
     can understand little of what the note-taker has written.  You notice
     some circles around several locations: Johannesburg, Harare, Murewa, 
     Victoria Falls, Bulawayo, Accra, Nairobi, Kampala, Durban, Abeokuta,
     and a number of other less definite marks and indentations around other
     locations.
