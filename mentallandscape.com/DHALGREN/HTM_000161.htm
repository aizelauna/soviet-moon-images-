Broadway (#161)

(an instance of Generic Urban Streen Scene made by Calkins)

     Tall brick buildings jab up into the bright haze and disappear. Most look abandoned, rain entering broken windows, but to the east is a high-rise apartment complex that looks like it is still being attended to. A sign above the door says LABRY APARTMENTS. To the west is the tallest office building in the city, a beautiful art-deco structure of marble and stainless steel. To the northwest is Bellona Auditorium, and to the northeast stands the O.o.f Theater.
