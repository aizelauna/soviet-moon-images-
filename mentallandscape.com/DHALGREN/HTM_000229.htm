Stone Building (#229)

(an instance of Generic Post-Apocalypse Room made by Calkins)

     To the north, across flat rock (puddles of rain water fill the depressions), is a building of black stones, rounded and the size of heads, webbed with white mortar. Above the building's several wings rises a square tower with a crenellated balcony of the same black stone. The vaulted windows, paned with pebbled glass, are deeply recessed. You hear waves crashing from the east.

[Citation: Dhalgren, pp 228,229.]

EXITS:
      [ west ] Clearing In The Park. You walk west along the winding gravel path.
      Looking across the expanse of flat rock, you see the steps leading up to the grand doors of the stone building. [ north ] Steps. You make your way across the level section of flat rock towards the building.
      A cluster of indistinct shapes swims in the glittering haze to the south. [ south ] Dying Moon Carnival. You look intently to the south, trying to capture the glittering lights in your gaze. You find yourself taking one step, then another...
      The path disappears into the trees to the east. [ east ] Desolate Shore. You wander into the trees and follow the path until emerging from the other side of the woods.
