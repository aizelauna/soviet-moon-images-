Lanya (#2377)

(an instance of Generic Scorpion Player Class made by Lanya)

     A young woman, barely into her twenties, more interesting looking than pretty. Her coppery hair and blue eyes are nice, though. She's wearing a work shirt that's starting to fray at cuffs and collar, jeans, and tall boots that lace up the sides with green leather thongs. From time to time, she blows a riff on a Hohner E blues harp and smiles at you over the silvery notes.

Lanya is carrying Hohner harmonica, rolled up blanket and copy of Been Down So Long It Looks Like Up to Me.

Go to location of this object, Toadstool.
