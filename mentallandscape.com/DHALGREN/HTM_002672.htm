Egg of Inexistence (#2672)

(an instance of generic room made by Cyrus)

      .
      There's no sound inside.
     
     A strange liquid fills everything, but you breath easly. The
     temperature is...perfect. Not cold, not hot, not even warm.
     Just perfect. So perfect you forget it.
     
      There's no light inside.
     
     Nothing to touch. Except the walls, but those are never near,
     and you end up forgetting about them. No floor, no ceiling.
     Just space...empty space, but for the liquid and the notion of
     you.
     
      There's you in here.
