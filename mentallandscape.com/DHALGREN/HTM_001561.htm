Triskelion Post-Industrial Park (#1561)

(an instance of Generic Post-Apocalypse Room made by Trismegistos)

     A thirty-meter square of Astroturf, bolted to the concrete surface of the Solitude, serves as a central court for a collection of squat buildings. In the middle of the artificial village green stands a flaming flagpole-beacon.
     The paved-over desolation of Dog Solitude stretches away to the north. Due south there is a filling station, and to the east you notice an open fire door in one of the buildings.

You notice some details:
