Cambrian Shore (#1072)

(an instance of Generic Post-Apocalypse Room made by Calkins)

     You are standing on the site of Bellona 500 million years ago, the shore of the sea. The air is breathable now, but a little thin. Behind you, desolate volcanic rock stretches as far as the eye can see. But before you is a tidal pool teaming with plants and animals. You stare in fascination at a variety of small creatures in the shallow pool -- most are totally unfamiliar and bizarre looking bugs. You notice a large worm with five eyes and a single clawed arm extending in front. You recognize a few trilobites with their large compound eyes, looking almost like wrap-around sunglasses.
     
     You look around and once again notice the word "next" chiseled in stone.
