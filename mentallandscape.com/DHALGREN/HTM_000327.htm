well, i didn't think i'd get this far, but here i am, asking for quota. i'm requesting 50k in order to flesh out the public building i've done; all of my original 50k has gone into public building (other than myself, weighing in at 4.4k, and a $thing with a :tell verb that helps me work on $exit messages). i think the public building i've done is worthwhile, and i'd like to do more.
my first project is a child of the generic wandering automaton. i've called it the generic wandering speaking automaton (gswa for short, #1044), because (obviously) it speaks. it's speak_msg property holds any number of strings, and responds with a random one when the command 'talk to ' is used. owners can also choose the way the gswa responds, since i've made a says_message property that allows the owner to make the gswa scream, mutter, giggle, etc. of course, these can be randomized as well. the gswa has 3 kids; one currently works in a bar, another is a mad guru who dispenses surreal bits of wisdom, and the other doesn't seem to have been programmed yet.
my other major public work has been the office building, southwest from legba's 'The Crossroads' (#113). currently the building is somewhat vacant; i live on the top (seventh) floor along with sick. the floor below is 'devi's temple', which houses my gswa guru. i hope that she will be a bigger attraction when i have more residents and services to offer in the building. the fourth floor is my storage area of sorts; i store the gswa here, and plan to keep other generics and the like here. floors two, three, and five are empty, although i may be adding residents soon. i also have an alternative to having residents on these empty floors; i plan on working on a tattoo/piercing parlour, and the idea of an art gallery has been suggested. i might take the art gallery idea and run with it, making it a gallery/coffee house, complete with poets, hippies, and the like.
i do have one contibuting resident in the building; soma is currently building a stairwell off the lobby, that will eventually lead to a basement. she has mentioned that she is considering building a network of tunnels from the basement. i am currently helping her with @messages and suggestions about building. neschek's home ('under the rock') is currently in my workroom, and i'm trying to convince him to come up with a few ideas we could work on together.
i invite you to come to the office building any time and wander about. all exits are described, and while there may not be too much to see right now, i think it's a good start. i have tried to remain in theme with my building, and it is publicly connected.
i hope you'll think, as i do, that i've put my first 50k to good use, and that you will see fit to grant me more. i would be happy to hear suggestions and comments, and if you feel this area isn't worth the quota, i'm willing to start fresh.
thanx for your time (and patience in reading this long long request),

Eclipse

Date: 1995 Mar 21, 11:28:38 a.m. PST
From: Avon (#2177)
To:   *Quota-Requests (#327)
Subj: Interzonal Edge Request

Steelgrave and I have finished a joint project, Morte Deliziozo (#584) with its refreshments and "owner", Mama Hunch (#997). Morte is now open to the public and we've used up our quota, but there are a few more touches we would like to make (Mama will become a GSWA with #1044's capabilities fully developed and a few more added, a mugwump will buttonhole people in a back alley, offering a certain unspecified but precious substance). But our real interest is in making a gambling & drug den. Watching how people on various MOOs have played with creating a hallucinatory unreality within the fantasy reality of MOO has interested us, and we'd like to try something a bit different. I'm only a beginning programmer, and Steelgrave even more so, but we have some conceptual innovations in mind. Out planned projects have this theme: to play with the boundaries between "reality" and "illusion" within the context of MOO. The virtual physicality of MOO gives rise to a "reality" here, and also its opposite, MOO illusion and fantasy. Defining and exploring these layers of illusion within illusion fascinates us. We want to build things that speak to the virtual senses, sometimes fooling them. In a slight way, you'll see this in the interaction of players and desserts in Morte ("taking" them so you have them while you eat them but so does someone else eating the same dessert, because no one really does). This is our try at answering problem of the limitations of bar-type drink verbs (like :order on Mama) vs. the less-than-interesting interaction with edibles made from recyclables. Our 4 desserts aren't the ultimate solution by any means, and we'd like to pursue this further.

Steelgrave and I both request more quota, since we put equal efforts into Morte and will be partners on future projects. If you have any questions or suggestions, we'll be glad to respond.

Avon
