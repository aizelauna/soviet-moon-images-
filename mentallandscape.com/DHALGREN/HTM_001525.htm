Danish Bar (#1525)

(an instance of The Club made by Redlance)

     You see an old room that has partly been repainted, but is not finished. Some new wirering hangs out from several places, and it has a fresh smell of sawdust. A couple of old workmen are running around. Trying to finish it in time.
     Next to one of the walls you see a small hatch in the floor.

EXITS:
      An old well used bamboo curtain hang here. Obscuring the view to the other room. [ BambooCurtain ] The Club. The bamboo hugs you allmost as if sorry that you already leaving.
      [ Hatch ] Old Giant Shoe.
