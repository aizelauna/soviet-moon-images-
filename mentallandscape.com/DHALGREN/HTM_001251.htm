Jupiter (#1251)

(an instance of generic thing made by Marcus) 

     
     High in the sky at about thirty-five degrees
     Mighty Jupiter shines brightly,
     With his garland of moons.
     
     From afar a silver shower comes down,
     Jupiter raining on Jupiter--
     All things turned over!
     
     The morning breaks in a glory,
     A string of pearls.
