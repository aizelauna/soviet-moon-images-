Jackson & Broadway (#164)

(an instance of Generic Post-Apocalypse Room made by Calkins)

     The intersection of Broadway and Jackson Avenue. The haze hides the day sky's variety, stains consciousness, covers the holocaust with something safe and insubstantial. This is what a *good* neighborhood in Bellona looks like? The ground floor windows are broken in the white houses around you; curtains hanging out. The street is clean but wet. Broadway runs north and south, and you see a ruined church to the northwest. Jackson continues to the east.

[Citation: Dhalgren, p 84.]
