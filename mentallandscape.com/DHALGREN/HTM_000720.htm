The Courtyard (#720)

(an instance of Generic Urban Streen Scene made by Pierce)

     You are on the front porch of the mansion, standing at its impressive gates. You see figures moving behind the curtains, but no noise exudes from the house. There are flowers of an African variety on either side of the footpath leading to the front door, smelling of honey and spices far away...
