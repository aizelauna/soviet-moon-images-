Quonset (#295)

(an instance of generic room made by Amber-Jessica)

     Echoing corrugated steel vault, dull reverb throb of the Honda portable
     generators outside. Mingled smell of gasoline and Love's Baby Soft. Halogen
     lamps hung from the support beams twist very, very slowly in the draft.
     
     Littered around the vast semicylinder's dirt floor are rusted metal cargo
     drums with a spray-stenciled shipping address: "Trend Industries, Monrovia,
     Liberia." Although decrepit, the drums are sealed, making it impossible
     to determine their contents. Several of them have been rolled away from
     the center of the floor to make room for a star-shaped pink linoleum
     cutout roughly three meters in diameter. Upon it you see Amber-Jessica's
     Hello Kitty slumber bag, which is surrounded by tattered back issues of
     _Tiger Beat_, _Mandate_, _Sassy_, and _Guns & Ammo_. The northern arm
     of the star points to a small gray industrial safe whose door is covered with
     glow-in-the-dark magnetic letters of various styles and sizes.
     
     A large and badly corroded hangar door to the south bears a hand-painted
     sign reading "DO NOT LEAVE FORKLIFTS RUNNING UNATTENDED."

You see Amber-Jessica here.
