Loading Porch (#206)

(an instance of Generic Urban Streen Scene made by Calkins)

     The streetsign on the corner lamppost says "Broadway". Across the rain-drenched street to the west you see a loading porch. From their tracks under the awning, four- and six-foot butcher hooks swing gently -- though there is no wind. A fire escape runs up beside the porch.
     To the east is the Bellona Bus Depot.

[Citation: Dhalgren pp 19,22.]

EXITS:
      You see the pylons of the bridge to the south. [ south ] Waterfront. You cautiously proceed south for several blocks.
      You see an overturned truck in the avenue to the north. [ north ] Stone Lions. You walk north several blocks, keeping an eye out doorways to duck into.
      Acrose the street, a fire escape runs up the side of a building. [ up ] Roof Top. You grab the bottom rung of the fire escape and climb up three stories to the roof.
      Above several revolving doors a sign read "Bellona Bus Depot". [ east ] Bellona: Bus Depot. The revolving doors are stuck, but since the glass is missing anyway, you just step through.
