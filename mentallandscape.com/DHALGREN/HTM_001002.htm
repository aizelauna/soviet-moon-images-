Cathedral Altar (#1002)

(an instance of generic integrating room made by Eclipse)

     Stripped of all it's finery, the altar still somehow seems to retain a touch of the holy. Shreds of red velvet are all that remain of the carpet that used to cover the steps to the altar. The kneeling pads that once surrounded the altar have been ripped into bits of cotton, and the wooden rails that once supported praying arms are long gone. A battered crucifix hangs above the altar from one chain, and screaming obscenities cover the altar itself and the walls around this space.
     The only space that seems untouched is directly behind the altar, where a pentacle is drawn in white chalk on the stone floor. Here, you see candle wax at certain points on the pentacle, and the scent of incense lingers. A few feet above you, an alcove in the wall houses a simple yet beautiful white marble statue of a woman, expression serene, arms outstreched. She has been dressed in a blue gown, trimmed with silver, and waisted with a silver belt. A crescent has been painted in blue paint upon her forehead.

EXITS:
      If you squint, you think you can make out a small chamber, but you aren't sure. [ south ] Preparation Chamber. You carefully head towards the dark room to the south.
      The gargantuan cathedral occupies your field of vision, from the ceiling to the floor to the doors. [ east ] Cathedral of Our Lady of the Stars. You make the long journey from the altar towards the front doors.
      [ booth ] Confession Booth. You quietly enter the confession booth.
