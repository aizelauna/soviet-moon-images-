Mausoleum (#2604)

(an instance of Generic Post-Apocalypse Room made by donnelly)

     This may have once been the tomb of some rich and dignified citizen but now it is covered in graffitti and ankle-deep in trash. Obscene slogans plaster the walls, and the coffins lie strewn on the floor, where the bones of their previous occupants mingle with the garbage. Loud muffled punk rock plays from somewhere beneath the stone floor. Down a short flight of steps lies a small crypt. The smell of rot, cigarettes and alcohol is almost overpowering. THrough the cracks in the walls the rain patters on the rubbish and the sunlight streams in..

You notice some details:
      Two ornate coffins, now lying empty on the floor. They look as if they were smashed apart by sledgehammers, or pickaxes.
      You see a collection of animal corpese, pornographic magazines, peices of rotting food, empty bottles, cigarette stubs, used contraceptives, badly copied version of Ginsberg and Kerouac, burnt books, swastika badges, spent candles and other unrecognisable matter.

EXITS:
      [ d ] A small Crypt. You take a breath and descend the stairs.
      [ out ] Graveyard. You shudder as you leave the Mausoleum

This place has a certain ambiance...
     A sudden wind disturbs the litter at your feet. Long dead film stars gaze with forlorn eyes from magazine covers.
     A shadow flits across the edge of your vision.
     The sound of a child crying rises from the crypt.
     You feel someone standing close behind you.
     A snake crawls inbetween copies of TIME magazine and the charred remains of Howl.
     A strange odour of sex and decay fills your nostrils.
     You hear the sound of distant voices shouting in foriegn tongues.
     A low mist seeps across the floor, disappearing into the crypt.
