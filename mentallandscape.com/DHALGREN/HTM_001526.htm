Storage Closet (#1526)

(an instance of generic integrating room made by Soma)

     You find yourself in a cramped room. Light spills down from a naked lightbulb about 8 feet above you. Shelves holding dust, old rags, a few empty boxes, a spare lightbulb and not much else line the back wall from floor to ceiling. A family of mice has made a home of the third shelf. A small cot, rusty and sans mattress, has been folded and leaned up against the shelves. A broken water heater is to the left of the shelves, and a dusty full-length mirror is to the right.

EXITS:
      You see a plain wooden door whose once bright blue paint is now cracked and faded with age. [ north ] Tiny Alcove. Feeling somewhat claustrophobic you go back into the alcove, which although it is small, offers a bit more space for movement. You close the closet door on your way out.
