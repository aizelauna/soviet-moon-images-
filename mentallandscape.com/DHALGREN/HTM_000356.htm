Caveman (#356)

(an instance of Generic Automaton made by Calkins)

     You see a short, stout man with long black hair and roughly made fur clothing. From the prominent brow ridge, massive nose and swept-back cheekbones, you suspect he is a member of the species _Homo neanderthalensis_. Thought to have become exinct in the late Pleistocene, he is a surprising find here!
     He is sleeping.

     [ feed ] tries to feed the Caveman. But she fails to get the attention of the sleeping Neanderthal.
     [ club ] raises her club and clobbers the sleeping Caveman. Now he is really sleeping!
     [ wake ] wakes the Caveman with a nudge. The Neanderthal eyes visitor warily, looking her up and down.
