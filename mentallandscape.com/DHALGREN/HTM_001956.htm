Desolate Shore (#1956)

(an instance of Generic Post-Apocalypse Room made by Calkins)

     You stand on a wide expanse of black-sand beach. To the east, ocean waves crash beneath the overcast sky. You see a few old wooden boats on the sand. Raising your hand to shade your view, you seem to see something far out in the water.

EXITS:

      You see some old but sea-worthy boats with oars. [ boat ] Sunken Islandia. You push one of the boats to the shore and into the water, hopping in as it clears the sand. You begin to row eastward.
