The River (#132)

(an instance of Generic Post-Apocalypse Room made by Calkins)

     You are treading water in the river, surrounded by floating trash. Newspapers, orange peels, used syringes. You imagine gangsters in concrete litter the riverbed below you. A concrete bridge pier rises out of the water just to the north, and you could probably swim over to it. The brown water is pelted by raindrops.
