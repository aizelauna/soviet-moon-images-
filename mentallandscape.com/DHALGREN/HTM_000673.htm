XXI. The Crown Of The Magi (#673)

(an instance of Generic Post-Apocalypse Room made by Calkins)

     You see the supreme Arcanum, a garland of golden roses surrounding a star and placed in a circle around which are set a equal distances the heads of a man, a bull, a lion and and eagle. This is the sign with which the Magus decorates himself when he has reached the highest degree of initiation and has thus acquired a power limited only by his own intelligence and wisdom.
     
     (proceed north)

EXITS:
      [ north ] Interzone Plaza. You stagger out of the gallary, and wander quietly back to the plaza.
