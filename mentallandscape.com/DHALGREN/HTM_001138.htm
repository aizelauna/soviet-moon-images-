Princess Mask (#1138)

(an instance of Generic Scripted Object made by Marcus)

      The mask is finely carved, of wood
      smooth and painted white. It shows
      the face of a fair princess, young
      but worn, with sorrow in her eyes.

Go to location of this object, Pattern Room.

After a moment, something begins to happen...
     
     The mask is a beautiful princess
      missing a lost lover.
     In her hands is whatever passion
      her face cannot show.
