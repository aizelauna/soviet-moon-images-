Roof Top (#89)

(an instance of Generic Post-Apocalypse Room made by Patroclus)

     You are standing on a rainingtarred roof several stories above the street. It is surrounded by a waist-high wall, grappled by the iron railings of a fire escape leading down. Beyond the brick balustrade, roofs and roofs checker the mist. A gap to the north may be a park. Beyond it is a hill, scaly with housing. You can glimpse the bridge to the west.
     Beyond a skylight to the west, you see a maintenance shack, put on the roof for tool storage. Bamboo curtains back recently puttied panes. The door -- tar paper has ripped in one place from greyed pine -- is ajar.

[Citation: Dhalgren, p 46.]

EXITS:
      You look over the cornice at the street, three stories below. [ down ] Loading Porch. You scramble over the cornice and climb down three stories of fire escape to the street.
      [ west ] Patro's Shack.
