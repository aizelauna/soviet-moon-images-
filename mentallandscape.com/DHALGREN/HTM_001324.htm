West Fox Terrace (#1324)

(an instance of generic room made by Sadao)

     This is the extreme western end of Fox Terrace. You can see a large grey marble structure to the north, the Palazzo Feldhyser, which rapidly nears completion. To the south is a verdant grove of trees, and nestled in its depths is a comfortable-looking house.
     To the west is The Park; Fox Terrace extends to the east, and at your feet is a glossy white stepdisc.
