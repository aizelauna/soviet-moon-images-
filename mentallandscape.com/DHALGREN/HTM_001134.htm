(an instance of generic room made by Boudicea)

     You are standing on a narrow footbridge just below water level and thus hidden by the surface. You can see the shore to the northeast, and a rather well-defended island to the southwest.

EXITS:
      The footbridge, as far as you can tell, runs right up to the palisade, so there must be a door there. [ sw ] crannog. You search the nearest portion of the palisade for a way in, and find a door. You enter, closing the door behind you.
