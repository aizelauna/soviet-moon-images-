Gaming Tables (#495)

(an instance of Generic Post-Apocalypse Room made by Calkins)

     A vast hill-top park overlooking the City of Interzone: minarets, palms, mountains, jungle...a sluggish river jumping with vicious fish. Gaming tables where the games are played for incredible stakes. From time to time a player leaps up with a despairing cry, having lost his youth to an old man or become Latah to his opponent.

[Citation: Naked Lunch pp 106-107.]
