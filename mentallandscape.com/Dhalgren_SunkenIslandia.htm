Sunken Islandia

You see now that the object visible from the beach was the top of a large oak tree emerging from the waters! Looking down you see the outlines of buildings and streets beneath the waves. With a chill, you realize you are floating above the watery grave of Islandia, one of the first great MUDs. Your gaze is drawn downward.
