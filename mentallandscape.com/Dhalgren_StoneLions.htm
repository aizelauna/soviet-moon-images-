Stone Lions

A truck is overturned at the block's end, and three cars, windows rimmed with smashed glass, squat on skewed hubs. To the east, there are no buildings: trees rise above the wall of the park. Stone lions flank the entrance. To the west, you see a cafe. A fire escape leads up its side. A trash bin sits on the corner.

The street continues to the north and to the south.
